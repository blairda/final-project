About

One of the largest outdoor arts festivals in the region, the Manayunk Arts Festival draws upwards of 200,000 attendees every year to Manayunk’s historic Main Street. Hundreds of local and national artists from around the country head to the annual event to sell handmade items of all sorts. The festival offers visitors the chance to browse a variety of goods while basking in a festival atmosphere full of food, music and other alfresco fun on open streets in Manayunk.

  • Unique Finds

    You’ll be able to score everything from walnut bowls to vintage threads to hand-crafted hammocks.

  • Delicious Food

    All of the restaurants and shops along Main Street are stationed right in the thick of the festival action.

  • Free Fun

    The festival is a completely free event for all to enjoy, with pay-as-you-go food, drink and shopping.

Look forward to extended spots for alfresco dining at restaurants along the street and food and drink specials, too.

Artists & Vendors

The festival’s artists and vendors feature seven different disciplines: Fiber, Glass & Ceramics, Jewelry, Mixed Media, Painting & Drawing, Photography, and Wood & Sculpture.

If you are interested in applying to join this year’s festival as an artist or vendor, please email mdouress@manayunk.org
